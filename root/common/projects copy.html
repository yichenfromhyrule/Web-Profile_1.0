Doodle Recognize System

Utilized: Python3, Convolutional Neural Network

oiseau, pájaro, 鸟, all these words mean 'bird', how do two different native languages people tell each other they are talking about 'bird'? They may point to a bird in the sky or just draw a bird. But if their drawing skill is really bad...This doodle system will fix this problem, as it has over 60,000 different doodles of 'bird' as the record. It also has records of 'sun', 'flower', 'clock', 'car'...over 10 normal items. It can recognize whatever you drew and return a item's image that they think you want to say.
